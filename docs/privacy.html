ChiSafeEats — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

ChiSafeEats does not collect, store, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no ads, and no trackers.

Location

If you grant location access, your location is used only on your device to center the map and sort nearby places. It is never transmitted, stored, or shared.

Data the app downloads

The app downloads public food-inspection records from the City of Chicago Data Portal. These requests contain no personal information.

Translation

Inspector comments are translated using Apple's on-device Translation framework. Text never leaves your device.

Storage

The inspection database and your settings (language, appearance) are stored only on your device, and are deleted when you delete the app.

Contact

For privacy questions, please contact us through the app's App Store listing.

ChiSafeEats — 隐私政策

更新日期：2026 年 6 月

ChiSafeEats 不收集、不存储、不分享任何个人数据。没有账号、没有统计分析、没有广告、没有任何追踪。

定位

如果你授予定位权限，位置信息仅在你的设备上用于定位地图和按距离排序附近店铺，绝不会被上传、存储或分享。

应用下载的数据

应用从芝加哥市公开数据门户下载公开的食品检查记录，请求中不包含任何个人信息。

翻译

检查员评语使用 Apple 设备端翻译框架翻译，文本不会离开你的设备。

存储

检查数据库和你的设置（语言、外观）仅保存在你的设备上，删除应用即全部删除。

联系方式

如有隐私相关疑问，请通过 App Store 应用页面与我们联系。
